### Weekly Structure:
- **Discussion Prompt**: Posted Monday
- **Independent Study**: Reading materials, videos
- **Peer Learning Circle**: Scheduled mid-week
- **Weekly Quiz**: Available Friday
- **Assignment Due**: Sunday

- **Wrap-Up & Feedback**: Sunday

### Content to Add:

#### Lessons:
1. **Introduction to Helping Profession**
   - Content: What is helping, roles and boundaries
   - Video: Role-play demonstration
   - Worksheet: Values clarification exercise

2. **Professional Ethics**
   - Content: Ethical principles, confidentiality, boundaries
   - Case Study: Ethical dilemma scenarios
   - Quick Reference: Core Ethical Principles Tool

3. **Cultural Competence**
   - Content: Nigerian cultural contexts, sensitivity
   - Resources: Cultural competency resources
   - Activity: Cultural awareness exercise

#### Discussion Prompts:
- "What motivates you to help others?"
- "How do cultural values influence helping in your community?"
- "Share an example of maintaining boundaries in helping"

#### Case Studies:
- Case 2: Mother with Grief (Amina)
- Case 3: Disability Stigma (Tunde)

#### Assignment:
- **Type**: Reflection
- **Title**: "Ethics and Cultural Competence Reflection"
- **Due**: End of week
- **Instructions**: Reflect on your motivations, ethical considerations, and cultural awareness

#### Quiz Questions:
- 10 questions on ethics, boundaries, cultural competence
- Passing score: 80%

#### Learning Journal Prompts:
1. What is one key insight you gained from this module?
2. How does this learning connect with your personal or professional experience?
3. What strengths did you notice in yourself during this session?
4. What challenges did you face, and how might you overcome them?
5. How will you apply this knowledge or skill in your daily life or community?
